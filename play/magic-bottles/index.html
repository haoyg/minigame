Magic Bottles is a color-sorting puzzle game built around planning several moves ahead. Move liquid between bottles, keep matching colors together, and clear each layout without trapping yourself.

How to Play Magic Bottles

Tap or click a bottle to select it, then choose another bottle to pour into. The main task is to sort colored liquid into matching bottles. Your goal is to complete each bottle with a single color. The main challenges are limited empty space and bottles that can only accept matching colors. Aim to separate every color cleanly before you run out of useful moves.

Game Controls

  • Keyboard: Mouse click or tap; keyboard is not required
  • Mouse or touch: Click bottles in sequence to pour matching liquid
  • Mobile: Tap, swipe, or drag depending on the game.

Tips and Tricks

  • Keep at least one bottle empty for temporary moves.
  • Do not bury a color under several unrelated layers unless you have a clear recovery move.
  • Finish one color at a time instead of moving every bottle at once.
  • If the board feels locked, undo mentally and look for the bottle with the fewest layers.
