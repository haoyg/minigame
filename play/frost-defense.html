Frost Defense is a cold-themed tower defense game built around slowing enemies and controlling paths. Upgrade carefully, place defenses where they can work longest, and stop waves before they break through.

How to Play Frost Defense

Use Click or tap to build, select, and upgrade defensive units. Your main task is to build frost defenses against enemy waves. Your goal is to protect your base through every wave. Watch out for stronger enemies, limited resources, and poor coverage areas and try to freeze, damage, and stop enemies before they reach the end.

Game Controls

  • Keyboard: Mouse or touch controls; keyboard shortcuts may vary
  • Mouse or touch: Click build points, upgrade options, and wave controls
  • Mobile: Tap, swipe, or drag depending on the game.

Tips and Tricks

  • Place slowing defenses where enemies stay in range longest.
  • Pair slow effects with higher damage towers.
  • Upgrade before the wave that usually leaks through.
  • Avoid spending all resources on one path too early.
