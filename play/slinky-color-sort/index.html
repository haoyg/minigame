Slinky Color Sort is a sorting puzzle where color order and empty space decide the solution. Move pieces between stacks, group matching colors, and avoid blocking yourself.

How to Play Slinky Color Sort

Click or tap a stack, then choose another stack to move pieces. The main task is to sort colored slinky pieces into matching stacks. Your goal is to group each color cleanly without mixing stacks. The main challenges are limited empty slots, blocked colors, and poor move order. Aim to finish every stack with the correct color grouping.

Game Controls

  • Keyboard: Mouse or touch controls; keyboard is not required
  • Mouse or touch: Click stacks in sequence to move colored pieces
  • Mobile: Tap, swipe, or drag depending on the game.

Tips and Tricks

  • Keep an empty stack available for temporary moves.
  • Finish one color at a time when possible.
  • Avoid burying a needed color under several others.
  • Move top pieces only when they create a clear follow-up.
