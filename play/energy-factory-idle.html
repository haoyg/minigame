Energy Factory Idle is an incremental strategy game about building production and reinvesting profits. Upgrade generators, improve output, and create a factory loop that keeps growing over time.

How to Play Energy Factory Idle

Use Click or tap buildings, upgrades, and production buttons. Your main task is to upgrade factory systems to increase energy output. Your goal is to grow production faster with each upgrade cycle. Watch out for slow early income, upgrade costs, and inefficient production chains and try to build a strong factory that scales without constant input.

Tips and Tricks
