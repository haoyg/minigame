Privacy Policy

Last reviewed: May 2026 · Written by the Pokopie Editorial Team

Pokopie is a simple HTML5 games portal. We try to keep data collection to the minimum necessary to operate the site and understand basic traffic patterns.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

Pokopie uses cookies and similar local storage technologies to remember your preferences (such as dark/light theme) and to understand how visitors use the site. We do not use these to track you across other websites.

Third‑party Cookies and Advertising

Google, as a third‑party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on Pokopie. Google’s use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve personalized ads to you based on your prior visits to this site or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info/choices or, for users in the EU, www.youronlinechoices.com. Additionally, many games on Pokopie are embedded from third‑party platforms such as GameDistribution. These providers may set their own cookies and collect data under their own privacy policies when you play their games.

You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings in your browser. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some site features.

Third‑party game providers

Many games on Pokopie are embedded from third‑party platforms such as GameDistribution. When you launch one of these games, the provider may set its own cookies, collect analytics, or show ads according to its own privacy policy and terms. By playing those games you agree to the respective provider’s policies.

Your choices
