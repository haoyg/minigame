Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Pokopie is a simple HTML5 games portal. We try to keep data collection to the minimum necessary to operate the site and understand basic traffic patterns.

Many games on Pokopie are embedded from third‑party platforms such as GameDistribution. When you launch one of these games, the provider may set its own cookies, collect analytics, or show ads according to its own privacy policy and terms. By playing those games you agree to the respective provider’s policies.

Cookies

Pokopie may use first‑party cookies or local storage to remember basic preferences such as your dark/light theme selection. We do not use these cookies to track you across other websites.

Your choices
